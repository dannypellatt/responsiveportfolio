Deep Dive


Project Brief:

Work with a team of developers to find the two furthest TacoBell locations.

Finds and collects the coordinates of all TacoBell locations within the continental US, then calculates the distances between them. The two furthest restaurants and their distance from one another is displayed on the console.

Technology Used:

ASP .NET Core || C# || MySQL || APIs || Test Driven Development || NuGet

Design:

As this application displays the results on the console, no design was necessary.

Metrics & Accolades:

Good feedback on our ability to work as a team, specifically splitting apart tasks, planning and presentation.
